Flourless Apple Cinnamon Muffins

Original recipe by Professor P, documented by her student

This recipe is a flourless cinnamon muffin perfect for the gluten-free people in your life!

I first had this dessert when I was a teacher's assistant for my wonderful college professor, Professor P. She is a tremendous baker, cook, teacher, and person. It was through the opportunity of being her TA that we fostered a beautiful friendship, and I consider her an incredibly close person in my life. She is family, which means her family is my family! Prof.P's hubsand is gluten-free, thus Prof P. has become an expert baker in all things GF and graciously shared this recipe with me.

These muffins will fool you. Their texture and taste are addicting, so much so that it's not even on your mind to ask if they are gluten-free, because your first question usually is "can I have more?"

Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond flours
  • 1 cup unsweetened apple sauce
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F degrees
  2. Blend eggs and vanilla
  3. Mix in all dry ingredients
  4. Add in maple syrup and apple sauce
  5. Transfer to a lined muffin tin
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes
